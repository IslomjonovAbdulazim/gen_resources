CarouselOptions nima?
height: Carousel balandligi (default: 400)

autoPlay: Avtomatik harakat (true/false)

autoPlayInterval: Harakat vaqti (Duration)

enlargeCenterPage: Markazdagi elementni kattalashtirish

viewportFraction: Har bir element kengligi (0.0-1.0)

enableInfiniteScroll: Cheksiz aylanish

Performance Optimization

Memory Management:
• dispose() method da controller'larni tozalang

Best Practices
Carousel uchun:
• viewportFraction 0.6-0.9 oralig'ida saqlang
• autoPlayInterval 3-5 soniya bo'lsin
• enlargeCenterPage foydalanuvchi e'tiborini jalb qiladi
• Controller orqali programmatik boshqaruv qo'shing

Indicator uchun:
• AnimatedSmoothIndicator CarouselSlider bilan ishlatng
• Dot o'lchami 10-16px oralig'ida bo'lsin
• onDotClicked orqali foydalanuvchi interaksiyasini qo'shing
• Rang kontrastiga e'tibor bering
